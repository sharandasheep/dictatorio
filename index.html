Dictators For Dummies

Dictionary

Dictator

/ˈdikˌtādər/

noun

A persuasive leader with the ability to brainwash their audience and make them do horrible things while remaining convinced that they are doing the right thing - even in killing. A dictator can control the people and their minds and alter their mindset, whether they favor the use of violence in it or not.

Fascism

/ˈfa-ˌshi-zəm/

noun

The state of a civilization in which a dictator is present, however not in complete control. Rebels remain, fighting against the authority, though they are likely to be executed for their refusal to obedience.
